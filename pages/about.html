About Snob Buster

Super Snob Buster - Version 1.0.0

This game is born from a conversation I overhear between a foreign intern with a local staff working at my office one fine day.

Foreign Intern : Hello..
Local Staff : Hi.. Where are you from ?
Foreign Intern : - mention a country name -
Local Staff : I hear its a big country.
Yeah it is.. Have you been there ?
No.. What takes you so far away here ?
I hear a lot about your country.
It's called archipelago, right ?
Yes.. We have at least a hundred islands.
No, actually it has only - mention a number -
How do you know that ?
Its your home country ! You should know better than I.
I grew up here, we never bother to know such things.
Its basic geography, and its your home country !
Do you know yours ?
Of course ! Ask away..
How many people live there ?
- mentions a number
... You cant possibly know every country...
Try me ...
!#@^$&%*

Apparently my local staff friend is not very good in Geography. He then approached me to challenge the snob intern. Since I am no good in Geography either, (also because there is a contest with IP 2 Location), this app is born.

Made in under a week, and only during spare time, I realize there are still many bugs & improvement. Do leave me a line for suggestion, feedback, word of encouragement, or donation would be very much appreciated
